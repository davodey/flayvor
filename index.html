Designed with simplicity

Lets face it, most components are complex. We wanted to build a library that’s easy to consume, accessible, with no build process required. All our Material and Bootstrap components can be used in any modern framework or, on any simple HTML page, with little to no configuration.

Material Components

Material design is one of the hottest design languages. That is why we went off the material design specification for all our material components. All components are easy to use and customizable, so you can find they flayvor you need for your site.

Bootstrap Components

No more copying 300 lines of code from the bootstrap doc page to paste on your page, just add the markup of the component you want. <flayvor-alert> </flavor-alert> Generates all the bootstrap markup, styling, and functionality, and its all self contained in the tag.
